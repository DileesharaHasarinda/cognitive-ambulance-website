Platform Overview

Built for the Real World of Emergency Response

Emergency medical services face critical delays caused by traffic congestion, non-compliant civilian drivers, ambulance crew fatigue, and incomplete patient information. The Cognitive Ambulance System is purpose-built to eliminate these failure points with edge-AI running directly on Raspberry Pi 5 — no cloud latency, no connectivity dependency.

Our crew monitoring module uses MediaPipe Face Landmarker with a quantized MobileNetV2 CNN and LSTM to deliver 90.28% accurate fatigue detection. The traffic clearance module achieves 87.92% behavioral prediction accuracy from smartphone sensors alone, with zero roadside hardware required.

Patient risk prediction combines QR-enabled digital health profiles with edge-AI classification, producing AI-generated clinical summaries for faster, better-informed hospital handover. All inference runs in under 50 ms on-device.

The pre-veterinary triage module fuses wearable pet vitals with owner-reported symptoms, generating urgency classification and a Pre-Vet Alert Package delivered to the clinic before the animal arrives. All four modules operate in parallel on a single low-power platform with offline fallback.
